## Context

Calibration is widely required and poorly explained. Much of what exists online is either marketing for a particular laboratory or standards text written for people who already understand it. Calibration.info sets out to explain the subject in plain English without losing technical accuracy.

## What I built

A clear, content-led reference site, drawing on long field experience in temperature metrology, that treats the reader as capable and gives them the context to ask better questions of their own calibration provider.

## Role

Writer and developer.

## Outcome

Live at [calibration.info](https://calibration.info/).

## What it shows

Technical communication as a craft: taking a regulated, jargon-heavy subject and making it genuinely useful to a non-specialist without overstating what calibration can and cannot promise.
