## Context

VintageRadio.info has been online since 1998, making it one of the longest-running projects here. Vintage radio sits at the meeting point of electronics, design history and craft; the site treats the subject with the care it deserves, drawing on a working life that began in electronics.

## What I built

A content website built around heritage and curation, where the visual presentation carries as much of the story as the text.

## Role

Creator. Built and maintained since 1998.

## Outcome

Live at [vintageradio.info](https://vintageradio.info/).

## What it shows

Interest-led content done properly: curation, heritage and visual storytelling, and a willingness to make something simply because it is worth preserving.
