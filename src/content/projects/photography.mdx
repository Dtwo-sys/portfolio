## Context

Photography is part of how the reflective and the technical sides of this work meet. I use it as a way to notice and capture details that often go unseen: coastal light, natural textures, architecture and everyday moments. The Northumberland coast images that run through Between the Waves come from here, as do the photographs that accompany the Liminal Light poetry collection.

## Role

Photographer. All images shot on iPhone.
