## Context

Between the Waves came out of a long mindfulness practice and counselling training. The aim was a meditation app with nothing in the way: no account to create, no subscription to weigh up, no tracking running quietly underneath. You open it and you press play.

## What I built

A free guided-meditation web app, installable as a progressive web app, offering six sessions for stillness, grounding and everyday wellbeing:

- Turning Inward (24 min)
- Grounding, 5·4·3·2·1 (8 min)
- Sleep, Yoga Nidra (17 min)
- Letting Go (7 min)
- Releasing (6 min)
- Acceptance (7 min)

Companion poems from *Liminal Light* and Northumberland coast photography run throughout, so the app draws the writing, the images and the practice into one place.

## Role

Designer, developer and narrator.

## Privacy and safeguarding

The app collects no personal data: no accounts, no tracking, no analytics. The sessions are free to stream; the full set can be downloaded through Ko-fi on a pay-what-you-want basis. The site also carries a clear note that the meditations are not a substitute for therapy or medical support, with crisis numbers. That responsible framing is part of the work, not an afterthought.

## Outcome

Live at [waves.calibratedideas.com](https://waves.calibratedideas.com/).

## What it shows

Calm, accessible web design and careful audio handling, held to a no-accounts, no-tracking ethos. It is the clearest single example of precision, care, writing and photography meeting in one project.
