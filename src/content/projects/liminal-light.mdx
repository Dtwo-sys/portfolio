## Context

Liminal Light was written to mark a 60th birthday: a collection of poems exploring themes of place, presence and change. Coastal imagery runs through it alongside personal reflection; the book captures moments of stillness, transition and hope. It is a deeply personal project and an example of carrying a creative idea through to print via self-publishing.

The poems and photographs are also the companion material that runs through Between the Waves — the two projects share the same sensibility.

## What I built

A poetry collection, self-published, with an accompanying website where the writing and the atmosphere around it are allowed to set the pace.

## Role

Writer and self-publisher. The poems are entirely my own words.
