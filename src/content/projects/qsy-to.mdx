## Context

"QSY" is amateur-radio shorthand for changing frequency. QSY.to is a short-link service built for that community, a small, focused utility rather than a general-purpose link shortener.

## What I built

A short-link service shaped around how radio enthusiasts actually share things, kept deliberately simple.

## Role

Creator. Built and maintained since around 1998.

## Outcome

Live at [qsy.to](https://qsy.to/).

## What it shows

A reading of a niche community's needs turned into a small, useful web service. Not every worthwhile project has to be large.
