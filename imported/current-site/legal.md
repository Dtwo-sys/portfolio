This is a personal blog, and your privacy matters to me.

I don’t collect more than I need, I don’t sell your data, and I won’t spam you — ever.

If you use the contact form, I may collect:

Your name and email – so I can reply to you.

IP address – for basic spam and security filtering.

Cookies – this site uses standard cookies required by WordPress and any plugins used for things like page performance or analytics.

None of this is used for advertising or shared beyond what's necessary to run the site.

See what data (if any) I have about you
