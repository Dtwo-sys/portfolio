## Paragraphs

Standing at the crossroads of technology, spirituality, and reflection. From metrology to AI, from ParkRun paths to crafted words and captured images, I seek dialogue between precision and presence. Here, I share my journey: building, exploring, creating—one calibrated idea at a time.

Hi, I’m David - though somewhere along the way the nickname D2 stuck. I’ve spent over 40 years in temperature metrology, working across calibration, training, technical writing, and international projects. Alongside the technical, I’ve always been drawn to creativity and meaning: exploring AI, developing digital tools, journaling, photography, and connecting with others through shared ideas.

In 2013, I trained as a counsellor — an experience that taught me the value of listening deeply: to people, to systems, and to myself. Through voluntary counselling and supporting online groups, I’ve found ways to contribute and stay connected to the wider community https://davidsouthworth.net

This space brings it all together: technology and tools, stories and creativity, and the sparks of curiosity that keep me learning.

I'm always happy to hear from people — if you have a question, need a hand, or just want to chat, feel free to get in touch
