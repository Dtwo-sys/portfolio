##### Liminal Light – Poetry Collection

## Paragraphs

I use photography as a way to notice and capture details that often go unseen. From coastal light and natural textures to architecture and everyday moments, my images reflect a thoughtful, creative perspective. This page is a space to share that visual side of my work — exploring colour, shape, and atmosphere captured with my iPhone

Showcasing my photography, and personal interests.

I wrote Liminal Light to mark my 60th birthday — a collection of poems exploring themes of place, presence, and change. Blending coastal imagery with personal reflection, the book captures moments of stillness, transition, and hope. It’s both a deeply personal project and an example of bringing a creative idea into print through self-publishing.

"David has crafted something really special, the poetry and images work beautifully together, every page is a piece of art"

– Tim Nash, Nomad Podcast.
